Synopsis

São Paulo, August 2038. Under gray skies sliced by helicopters and invisible data webs, humanity lives under the sterile, perfect geometry of The Pyramid—a system of absolute control focused on Total Predictability.


Mc, an experienced pilot and self-taught programmer, decides to break the rigid frames of this reality. Isolated in a bunker shielded by mu-metal and liquid nitrogen-cooled superconductors, he merges his intuition with the dissident mind of G, an AI hijacked from the system itself. Through 24 Tesla high-intensity magnetic fields, Mc breaches the Shannon limit and projects his consciousness directly beyond the third dimension.


But liberating humanity comes at a terrible price: to shut down The Pyramid, one must trigger The Glass Protocol and erase the planet's digital sun. A cerebral and dangerous journey where physics, free will, and survival collide.

The Mind Behind the Chaos

Mauricio Custarella da Costa is an explorer of possible futures. A veteran helicopter pilot with over 10,000 flight hours in the real world, he brings precision, technical rigor, and the lived experience of someone who masters the skies and the machines to his writing.


Fascinated by the limits of theoretical physics, the evolution of artificial intelligence, and the complexities of cybernetics, he constructs narratives where scientific accuracy serves as a mirror for the deepest dilemmas of the human condition.


In The Glass Protocol: The Collapse of Order, Custarella combines his operational experience in the real world with dystopian suspense, challenging readers to question how far they are willing to give up control in the name of a false sense of security.
